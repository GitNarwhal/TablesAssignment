About Skylar!

I love to code, and while it's still quite confusing to me, it's about the learning process and improvement for me more than the end products themselves. Reasons I like to code include:

  • The amount of programs, games, or websites you can make are limitless
  • Seeing logic being compiled and built, then shown in the digital world is very satisfying
  • Coding is not just logical, but can also be a very fulfilling creative process as well
  • I would like to make a program that is useful to others and can make a positive impact on others' lives
